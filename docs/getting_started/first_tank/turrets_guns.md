# Turrets
Turret entities are the primary method for aiming things on your vehicle.

Spawn a 72 unit horizontal turret from the menu and place it on your vehicle.

Spawn a 24 unit vertical turret from the menu and place it at the front of your horizontal turret ring.

# Guns
Guns can be used to damage other vehicles.

Spawn a 100mm gun and place it like so.

Next, parent it to the vertical turret ring.
