Waterjets allow you to move in water.

They can be linked just like wheels can to a gearbox.

A positive total gear ratio results in forwards movement.

A negative total gear ratio results in backwards movement.

The `Pitch` and `Yaw` inputs (from -1 to 1) can be used to "steer" the output of the waterjet, similar to vector thrusting.
